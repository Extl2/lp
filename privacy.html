Privacy Policy

Grand Eternity Ginza Clinic ("the Clinic", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information and protected health information ("PHI"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our website or our medical services, including telemedicine services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Address
  • Email address
  • Telephone number
  • Medical history
  • Current medications
  • Consultation records
  • Payment information
  • Shipping information for prescription medications

2. Purpose of Use

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing medical care and telemedicine services
  • Determining medical eligibility for prescription medications
  • Processing payments
  • Shipping prescription medications within Japan
  • Scheduling appointments
  • Responding to patient inquiries
  • Complying with applicable laws and regulations

3. Telemedicine Services

Telemedicine services are provided only by licensed physicians in Japan and only to patients located within Japan in accordance with applicable Japanese laws and Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare guidelines.

4. Prescription Medications

Prescription medications are dispensed only after a live medical consultation with a licensed physician.

Prescription medications are shipped only to addresses located within Japan.

5. Protection of Personal Information and PHI

We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information and protected health information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Access to patient information is limited to authorized personnel who require the information for legitimate medical or administrative purposes.

6. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not sell personal information.

Information may be disclosed only:

  • when required by law;
  • with patient consent;
  • to payment processors;
  • to shipping providers for prescription delivery;
  • to service providers assisting our operations under confidentiality obligations.

7. Data Retention

Medical records are retained in accordance with applicable Japanese medical laws and regulations.

8. Your Rights

Patients may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information where permitted by applicable law.

9. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and analytics technologies to improve website functionality and user experience.

10. Applicable Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of Japan, including the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI).
